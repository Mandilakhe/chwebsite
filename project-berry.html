Raspberry Pi

The entrepreneurs are also working on Raspberry pi. The Raspberry Pi is a credit-card sized computer board
that plugs into a monitor or TV and a mouse and keyboard. It’s a miniature ARM-based PC which can be
used for many of the things that a desktop PC would, like spreadsheets, word-processing and games. It also
plays High-Definition video. The range now includes the greatly improved second generation Raspberry Pi 2
products low power & low cost Raspberry Pi Model A+, and the Raspberry Pi Compute Module for embedded
applications as well as a variety of accessories to support a wealth of choices for our customers based on
their individual application needs. Suitable whether you want to learn to programme, hack around with some
hardware, use it for prototyping or design it in at the core of your innovative new device.
